Your carbon footprint

Your carbon footprint is made up of your diet, transportation choices, home size, shopping and recreational activities, usage of electricity, heating, and heavy appliances. To calculate your footprint you will be asked several questions around these topics, and based on your answers an estimate of your own footprint will be calculated.

Why Register with us

It is good to be aware of how much CO2 you personally contribute, however it is in your best interest to have a more complete image. By registering you can keep a log of your monthly carbon footprint, helping you take control of your own carbon emissions. You will also be get access to upto date news and information surrounding our environment and planet.
